About Us

A computer science and coding club for student's run by students.

Our Values:

  • Teamwork: working together to create
  • Creativity: imagining to design
  • Leadership: communicating to success

Our Mission:

Punahou School's 'Enehana - Computer Science and Coding - Club's goal is to create a space where those who are interested in computer science are able to explore and discover various areas of interest, regardless of their techspertise. Our mission is to have a gathering space for those who have gained/have an interest in technology where they can share ideas and knowledge. 'Enehana Club can also help those around campus, such as other clubs and classes, and other organizations create ways to better connect with each other.
